    A note on the defensive numbers. The on-ice “against” stats (xGoals Against/60) reward sheltered usage; a bottom-six player who mostly faces weak competition can post strong suppression numbers. It is the best team-defense signal in this data, but not a pure individual-skill measure.

    Percentiles are ranked within position; forwards are compared against forwards, defensemen against defensemen, and goalies against goalies. Qualifiers: skaters need >200 minutes or >20 games played; goalies need >15 games or >600 minutes. Grey (-) goalie bars are workload context; they show how hard the shots faced were, neither good nor bad.

    Goalie shutouts and win–loss records are not in the MoneyPuck shot data, so the summary shows GAA, save percentage and games played. Stats from MoneyPuck; headshots and logos from the NHL. 2025–26 regular season.
